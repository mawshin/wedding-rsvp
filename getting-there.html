Maw Shin & Anna Wedding

There are a few ways to get to the location via public transport, we have listed a few below for reference:

From Yishun (Google map link)

  1. Alight at Yishun MRT
  2. Head over to Northpoint City
  3. Exit at North Wing (near to Encik Tan)
  4. Look out for the bus stop with Bus 117 after exit on the left and alight at the 20th stops [Aft Seletar A'space Rise]
  5. Walk to the Summerhouse (4 mins walk)

From Khatib (Google map link)

  1. Alight at Khatib MRT
  2. Cross overhead bridge to the bus stop opposite
  3. Look out for Bus 117 and alight at the 17th stops [Aft Seletar A'space Rise]
  4. Walk to the Summerhouse (4 mins walk)

From Punggol (Google map link)

  1. Alight at Punggol MRT
  2. Head over to Punggol temporary bus interchange
  3. Board Bus 117 and alight at the 8th stops [Bef Seletar A'space Rise]
  4. Walk to the Summerhouse (5 mins walk) across the road

From Serangoon (Google map link)

  1. Alight at Serangoon MRT
  2. Head over to Serangoon interchange
  3. Board Bus 103 and alight at the 24th stops [Bef Seletar A'space Rise]
  4. Walk to the Summerhouse (5 mins walk) across the road
